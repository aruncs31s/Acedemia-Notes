### Advantages of State-Space Approach
1. Applicable to **multi-input multi-output (MIMO)** systems.
2. Can represent **time-varying and nonlinear systems**.
3. Includes **initial conditions** directly in the model.
4. Suitable for **higher-order systems**.
5. Provides complete information about internal system states.
6. Enables analysis of **controllability and observability**.
7. Well suited for **computer-based and modern control design**.
