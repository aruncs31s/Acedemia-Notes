## Two-Ray Model

For ground-reflected signals (near ground):

$$P_r = P_t G_t G_r \frac{h_t^2 h_r^2}{d^4}$$

- $h_t$ = Transmit antenna height
- $h_r$ = Receive antenna height

**Breakpoint distance:**
$$d_{bp} = \frac{4 h_t h_r}{\lambda}$$

| Region | Path Loss Exponent |
|--------|-------------------|
| Free space (d < d_bp) | 2 |
| Two-ray (d > d_bp) | 4 |
