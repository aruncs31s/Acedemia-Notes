# Surface Wave (Ground Wave) Propagation

Surface wave propagation refers to radio waves that travel **along the surface of the Earth**, closely following its curvature.

---

## Frequency Range
- [Frequency_Range:: **15 kHz – 2 MHz**]

---

## Characteristics
- [Characteristic:: Wave travels along Earth's surface]
- [Characteristic:: Strong interaction with ground]
- [Characteristic:: Requires antennas close to ground]

👉 Signal continuously loses power due to **ground absorption**

---

## Limitations
- [Limitations:: High attenuation with distance]
- [Limitations:: Not efficient at high frequencies]

---

## Applications
- [Applications:: AM Radio (500 kHz – 1500 kHz)]
- [Applications:: Long-distance low-frequency communication]
